Detailed Description

A Bounding Container which is in the shape of an octagon.

The OctagonalEnvelope of a geometric object is tight along the four extremal rectilineal parallels and along the four extremal diagonal parallels. Depending on the shape of the contained geometry, the octagon may be degenerate to any extreme (e.g. it may be a rectangle, a line, or a point).
